Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first AI agent?
Navigate to the Agents page and click "Create New Agent". Follow the setup wizard to configure your agent's personality, knowledge base, and integrations.
Can I schedule content across multiple platforms?
Yes! Connect your social media accounts in the Integrations section, then use the Schedule page to plan and publish content across all platforms.
What integrations are supported?
AI Cora supports Twitter/X, Instagram, LinkedIn, Discord, WhatsApp, WordPress, YouTube, and more. Check the Integrations page for the full list.
How do I upgrade my plan?
Go to Settings > Billing to view available plans and upgrade your subscription.
